Make beautiful graphs with R

Wednesday, Jun 12, 2019 by David Odongo ggplot2, bbplot, dplyr

Greetings to you my friend, today i would like to talk about how to make charts in R. We shall use the ggplot2 package which follows the rules of grammar of graphics. A grammar of graphics is a tool that enables us to concisely describe the components of a graphic. Such a grammar allows us to move beyond named graphics (e.g., the ``scatterplot’’) and gain insight into the deep structure that underlies statistical graphics
